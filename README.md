**Introduction:**
This project was developed as part of the Student Digital Ambassador role. The objective is to create a comprehensive Digital Literacy Portfolio that serves as a guide for peers to navigate the digital world responsibly and professionally. The portfolio is organized into five distinct tasks, each corresponding to a core module of the course.
**Module Summaries**
**Task 1:** Digital Literacy Awareness Infographic – A visual resource created to explain the importance of digital literacy and safe internet practices.
**Task 2:** Student Digital Portfolio – Establishment of a professional presence across key platforms such as GitHub and LinkedIn.
**Task 3**: Coding & Collaboration Platforms – An exploration of online tools used for studying, practicing, and collaborating in a technical environment.
**Task 4:** Professional Email Etiquette – Development of effective communication skills through sample drafts and etiquette checklists.
**Task 5:** Cybercrime Awareness – A study on digital safety, including a case study write-up and prevention strategies.
